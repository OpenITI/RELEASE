## 1. Describe why you chose this specific version of the text. Why others, in your opinion, are worse?

The JK version has no pagination. Of the origin of the Shia version little could be deduced. The Shamela version is clear: it is Muhammad Abu al-Fadl Ibrahim's edition, which is largely based on the Leiden edition. It has clear pagination and none of the weird bracketing the SHia version shows.

## 2. Which edition you used for collation? How close the text to the edition? Is pagination the same?

Dar al-Ma'arif bi-Misr, same pagination. Basically the same.
